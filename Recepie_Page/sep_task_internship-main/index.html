Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving Size: 8 servings

Show Ingredients
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
Show Steps
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  • Stir together flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  • Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  • Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour into prepared pans.
  • Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely.
